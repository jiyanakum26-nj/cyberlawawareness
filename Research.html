Abstract / Summary

  • The study examines cyber-law awareness among small business owners using digital technologies.
  • Findings show most owners have limited knowledge of cyber-law and legal compliance.
  • Low awareness increases risks of data breaches, financial penalties, and reputational damage.
  • Key causes include lack of affordable legal guidance and low prioritization of cyber risks.
  • The study recommends education, accessible compliance resources, and proactive cybersecurity practices.

Key Insights

  • Cyber crimes are increasing due to lack of awareness among users.
  • Strong cyber laws and strict enforcement reduce online fraud and identity theft.
  • Awareness programs significantly improve online safety behavior.
  • Reporting cyber crimes is crucial for timely intervention and legal action.
