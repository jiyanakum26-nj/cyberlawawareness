Cyber Law Awareness

Cyber Law governs the legal issues related to the use of computers, networks, and the internet. This website aims to educate people about cyber laws, cyber crimes, and safe digital practices.

Introduction

Cyber Law refers to the legal framework that governs activities on the internet, computer systems, and digital communication. With the rapid growth of technology, cyber laws play a crucial role in protecting individuals, organizations, and governments from cyber crimes and misuse of digital platforms.

Objectives of Cyber Law Awareness

  • To create awareness about cyber laws and digital rights.
  • To educate users about cyber crimes and legal consequences.
  • To encourage safe and ethical use of the internet.
  • To protect individuals from online fraud, identity theft, and cyber attacks.

Scope of Cyber Law Awareness

  • Protection of personal data and online privacy.
  • Prevention of cyber crimes such as hacking and phishing.
  • Legal regulation of e-commerce and online transactions.
  • Recognition of digital signatures and electronic records.
  • Ensuring cybersecurity and responsible digital behavior.
